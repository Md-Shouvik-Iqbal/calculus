Calculus

The author—Md. Shouvik Iqbal—was born and raised in Bangladesh and completed his secondary (SSC) and higher secondary (HSC) education there. Besides his academic research, he also has a longstanding engagement with creative disciplines, earning him awards at both national and international levels.

The book—Calculus by Md. Shouvik Iqbal—began as a collection of notes when he was 16 and is now published at the age of 19, making him among the youngest to publish an open-access textbook.

If you appreciate the effort that went into this project and believe in the importance of making educational resources available to all, you can show your support in several ways. Contributing to this initiative helps maintain the quality of the work and supports the author’s continued efforts to create accessible educational content. Whether it’s through a monetary donation or simply spreading the word, your support will ensure that this work can continue and that more resources like this can be created.
